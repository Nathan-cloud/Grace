Who We Are

Our history

We opened our doors in April of 2018 when our highly recommended & experienced team heard the Permian Basin community. Parents were wanting something new and different

So, AmaZing Grace opened the new Early Learning Center that offers great customer service, Christian values, early learning for your kids and so much more! Our Early Education staff works with professional quality so that we can provide our services. We work the hours and days to help our hard working & college student family clients. Much of our staff has years, accumulating to decades, of childcare workplace experience. Our early education tuition rates are highly competitive in the Permian Basin Area!

Word From Our Founder

Step into a world of wonder at AmaZing Grace! Here, we're not just caregivers; we're storytellers, explorers, and friends. Our mission is to nurture your child's uniqueness, ignite their curiosity, and foster friendships that last.
